About Me

I started my work with software development in 2009, as a intern at Signove SA, for one year. During this time I worked with Web Development, using Grails and UI test, using Selenium.

After that, I worked for one more year with web development in other places, where I improved my knowledge in this area using HTML, JavaScript and CSS, until finish my Bsc, in 2012.

In 2013 I started my Msc at Federal University of Ceara, where I worked with software quality and measurements in ubiquitous systems. During this time, I also learned how to make a cientific research and how to improve my writting.
